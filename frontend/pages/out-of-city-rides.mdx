Looking for a safe ride to get you somewhere out of Miami? Executive Limo Service offers safe, affordable long distance limo and car service. Wherever you want to go we will get you from point A to point B without any distractions. You will enjoy the best possible service and get on desired location on time. Point to point service is set up in advance so you don’t have to worry about price changing. All you have to do is send a request by filling out the form below this text or just call us. Our customer service will listen to your requests and fully commit to customize your ride specifically to your requirements. We will do all possible to accommodate all your needs and special requests. 
If you want us to wait for you and take you back to point A or add one or more stops to your trip don’t hesitate to call. We will make sure to advice you on the most cost-effective option for your needs.
